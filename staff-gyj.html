BIOGRAPHY

Yongji Guan was born in 1991 in Huining county, Gansu Province, China. He graduated with a B.S. degree from Lanzhou University in 2014. In 2019, he received his Ph.D. degree (supervised by Prof. Xiaoping Zhang and Youquan Deng) on the nanowetting and energy harvesting of ionic liquids using computational calculations from school of Information Science and Engineering, Lanzhou University.

From 2019 to 2021, he worked as a postdoctoral research fellow at department of chemistry in Imperial College London, advised by Prof. Tom Welton. Then he was appointed as an associate professor at school of Information Science and Engineering in Lanzhou University.
