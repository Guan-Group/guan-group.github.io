Undergraduate students at School of Information Science and Engineering are encouraged to discuss with me about potential research projects. I am also interested in hosting visiting students.

Ionic liquids, also known as room temperature ionic liquids (RTILs), have been widely applied in various fields due to excellent and unique physicochemical properties. Investigating the micro-structures and properties of ionic liquids is very significant and promising to further promote application. My research interests focus on the structures and dynamics of ionic iquids on the nanoscale using computational calculations. Meanwhile, i also study the microwave adsorption of ionic liquids by experimental method.

Research Interests

  • The structures and dynamics of ionic liquids in external force

  • The nanowetting behaviour of ionic liquids/mixtures

    • Natural wetting

    • Electrowetting

  • Microwave adsorption of ionic liquids

  • Vibrational spectrum of ionic liquids
